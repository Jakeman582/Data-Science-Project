USA: #1 Pop Songs, 1950-1959

USA’s #1 Pop Songs:

1950-1959

These are the #1 pop songs on the United States pop charts from 1950 to 1959. Songs could have hit #1 on any of the following charts:

  • Billboard Hot 100 chart, 1958-present (BB)
  • Billboard Best Sellers chart, 1940-1958 (BS)
  • Billboard Juke Box Hits, 1940-1957 (JB)
  • Billboard Disc Jockey Hits, 1944-1958 (DJ)
  • The Gavin Report, 1958-2000 (GR)
  • Your Hit Parade, 1935-1959 (HP)
  • Cashbox, 1950-1996 (CB)
  • Hit Records (Music Vendor/Record World), 1954-1982 (HR)
Numbers following the letter codes indicate how many weeks the song spent at #1 on that chart. Links go to specific lists of the biggest songs of all time for that chart. Your Hit Parade charts and early Cash Box charts did not indicate specific artists, so the artists identified here are those which also hit #1 on another chart or are the highest-ranked version according to Dave’s Music Database.

Also, when “BB” appears in blue it indicates a link to the Stereogum.com series on Billboard #1 songs.
